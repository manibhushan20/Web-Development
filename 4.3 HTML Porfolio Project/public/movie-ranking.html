The Best Movies According To Manibhushan

My top 3 favorite movies of all time:-

1. Pursuit Of Happiness

Beautiful with my favorite actor will smith in lead character. This movie really touches us, its very emotional movie shows the reality of life. It shows it's not easy to earn living in this world.

2. 3 Idiots

It's beautiful film on college life especially engineering college. It teaches many life lessons to us.

3. Intersteller

Best science fiction movie. It explores the complex part of science like quantum physics, time travel and black hole.
